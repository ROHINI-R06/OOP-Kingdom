# 👑 Contributor Ranks

As you contribute to the OOP Kingdom and your Pull Requests get merged, you will ascend the ranks of the kingdom. Your rank is a testament to your architectural prowess and dedication to the realm.

| Rank | Title | Requirement | Description |
| :---: | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 🌱 | **Settler** | 1st merged PR | You have staked your claim in the kingdom. Your first building stands! |
| ⚒️ | **Craftsman** | 3 merged PRs | You are a recognized builder. The citizens rely on your steady hands. |
| 🏛️ | **Architect** | 7 merged PRs | Your designs shape the skyline. You are a master of Object-Oriented design. |
| 👑 | **Royal Council** | 15 merged PRs | You sit among the kingdom's elite. Your word is law in matters of architecture. |

Keep building, and your name will echo through the chronicles of the OOP Kingdom forever!
